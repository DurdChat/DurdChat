Durd

Talk to, role-play with, and rate others!

Your Rating:

With Durd, you can chat or role-play about almost any topic you want and meet new people. After every session, you can rate the other person on a scale from -5 to 5 that will impact his/her rating. You will be matched with someone of a similiar rating. All chats are completely anonymous unless you give out your information (not recommended). You can stop a chat at any time, but remember that the other person can impact your rating. Durd is a great way to improve your social skills. And always remember! Don't be "durdy" or you will be downvoted to oblivion.
