With Durd, you can chat or role-play about almost any topic you want and meet new people. After every session, you can rate your partner on a scale from -5 to 5 that will impact his/her rating. You will be matched with someone of a similiar rating. All chats are completely anonymous unless you give out your information (not recommended). You can stop a chat at any time, but remember that your partner can impact your rating.
